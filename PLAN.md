## Feature description

Build the leave management module. Employees apply for annual, sick, and emergency leave. Managers approve or reject. System tracks balances.

- **employee** (`src/modules/employee`) — owns: employee.model.ts, employee.repository.ts, employee.service.ts, employee.routes.ts

## Domain entities
- **LeaveRequest** — Tracks leave requests made by employees, including their status and associated details. (attributes: id, employeeId, type, startDate, endDate, status, managerId, managerComment, createdAt)
- **LeaveBalance** — Maintains the leave balance for each employee, tracking total and used leave days. (attributes: employeeId, leaveType, totalDays, usedDays, year)
- **LeavePolicy** — Defines the policies governing different types of leave, including limits and approval requirements. (attributes: id, leaveType, defaultDaysPerYear, maxConsecutiveDays, requiresApproval, createdAt)
